6-Week Weight Loss Meal Plan

Daily Targets

  • Protein: 120–180g
  • Water: 2–4L
  • Calories: Moderate deficit

Sample Day

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt (250g) + oats (30g) + berries
  • Lunch: Chicken (150g) + rice (150g cooked) + vegetables
  • Dinner: Salmon or chicken (150g) + potatoes (250g) + salad
  • Snack: Fruit + protein yogurt or shake

Tips

  • Eat protein with every meal
  • Avoid sugary drinks
  • Stay consistent, not perfect
