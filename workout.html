6-Week Workout Plan

Weekly Structure

  • 3–4 Strength Training Days
  • 2 Cardio Days
  • 1 Rest Day

Example Weekly Split

Day 1 – Upper Body

  • Push-ups – 3 x 10–15
  • Dumbbell rows – 3 x 12
  • Shoulder press – 3 x 10

Day 2 – Cardio

  • 30–45 min brisk walk or jog

Day 3 – Lower Body

  • Squats – 3 x 12
  • Lunges – 3 x 10 each leg
  • Calf raises – 3 x 15

Day 4 – Rest / Light Walk

Day 5 – Full Body

  • Burpees – 3 x 10
  • Plank – 3 x 30–60 sec
  • Jump squats – 3 x 12

Day 6 – Cardio

  • HIIT (20 min intervals)

Day 7 – Rest
