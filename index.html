Arnold Split Workout Program

Monday – Chest & Back

Chest

  • Incline Smith Machine Press – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Machine Chest Press – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Pec Deck – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Back

  • Machine Upper Back Row – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Machine Lat Row – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Lat Pulldown – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Tuesday – Arms & Shoulders

Triceps

  • Tricep Pushdowns – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Biceps

  • Preacher Curl – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Single-Arm Bayesian Curl – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Forearms

  • Cable Rope Hammer Curls – 2 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Cable Reverse Curls – 2 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Cable Forearm Curls – 2 sets × 6-8 reps

Shoulders

  • Cable Lateral Raises – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Shoulder Press – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Wednesday – Legs & Abs

Quadriceps

  • Leg Press (Quad Focus) – 2 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Leg Extensions – 3 sets × 6-8 reps

Hamstrings

  • Hamstring Curls – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Stiff-Leg Deadlifts (SLDLs) – 2 sets × 6-8 reps

Glutes, Adductors & Calves

  • Hip Thrusts – 2 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Adductor Machine – 3 sets × 6-8 reps
  • Calf Raises – 4 sets × 6-8 reps

Abs

  • Cable Ab Crunches – 3 sets × 8-12 reps
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets × 8-12 reps
